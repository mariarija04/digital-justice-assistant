IMPORTANT: Agreements for temporary employment are based on contingent requirements and are primarily used as a stop-gap arrangement. It is important to ensure compliance with relevant laws.

Mr. (Name)

Sub: Temporary appointment.

You are appointed on the temporary post of ——————— till further orders/for a fixed term on a fixed term purely on a temporary post from ———————- to ————————– subject to the following terms and conditions:

  1. Your appointment is purely temporary. The employment is only for the fixed term as mentioned above and shall automatically come to an end after the period specified above, and you will have no claim or right to the post thereafter.
  2. Your fixed emoluments per month will be Rs—————-, and you will not be entitled to any other allowance, etc., over and above the same. You will, however, be given all other statutory benefits of ESI, EPF, etc., as applicable to you.
  3. In the event the management decides to extend your services further, the same shall be done with a written communication to you. Unless extended via a specific written communication, your services shall not be required after the date mentioned above.
  4. Your appointment is at present for the unit at ———————. However, your services are liable to be transferred to any present or future branch/unit/sister concern of the company at any time, depending upon the requirements of the manpower. Since we provide various ancillary services to various export houses, you may be deployed at any of our client's establishments for executing the jobs therein.
  5. Your services are liable to be terminated without assigning any reason at any point in time during the tenure of your employment with us.
  6. You will be bound by the rules and regulations governing the management and maintain complete discipline in the organization.

Please sign the duplicate copy of this appointment letter as a token of your acceptance of the terms and conditions of your appointment with us.

For

(Management) (Employee)
